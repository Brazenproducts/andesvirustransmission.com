How to clean without making exposure worse

The biggest cleanup mistake is stirring contaminated dust into the air. Safer cleanup is slower, wetter, and less dramatic than panic-cleaning.

Basic sequence

  • Ventilate first
  • Do not dry sweep
  • Wet contaminated areas with disinfectant
  • Use gloves and respiratory protection
  • Bag waste carefully

Why this matters

Rodent-related exposure remains the main transmission concern, so cleanup behavior matters more than internet speculation.

Useful supplies

N95s, nitrile gloves, and disinfectants are the most relevant basics for most cleanup scenarios.
